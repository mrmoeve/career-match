Procurement Agent
PulteGroup
Bridgewater Township, NJ

The Procurement Agent manages procurement and community setup, combining sourcing and operational strategies to ensure efficient and effective execution.
This role includes managing supplier relationships, sourcing initiatives, category management, community-specific procurement needs, compliance, process accuracy, and budget efficiencies.
Responsibilities include supplier performance, category data entry, pricing validation, procurement policy and procedures for new communities, cost analysis, negotiation preparation, contract implementation, trade partner strategy, reporting, operational accuracy, risk management, value engineering, and continuous improvement.
Required skills include procurement systems, community setup systems, category management, supplier relationships, construction industry practices, cross-functional communication, analytical support for cost analysis, and compliance.
